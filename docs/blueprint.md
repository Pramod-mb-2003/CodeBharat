# **App Name**: Interest Ignition

## Core Features:

- Interest Psychometry Quiz: Administer a psychometric quiz to determine the user's top interests among sports, science, English, creativity, social studies, and math. The provided link offers examples.
- Interest Assessment with AI Tool: Use an LLM as a tool to accurately analyze quiz responses and identify the user's top 2-3 interests based on their responses. Pass the answer from Psychometry quiz to obtain the accurate top 2 or 3 interests.
- Interest Confirmation/Editing: Allow the user to confirm or edit the AI-selected interests from the initial list of six, ensuring accuracy and personalization before proceeding. Users are allowed to edit their preferences by selecting 2 or 3 interests before clicking confirm.
- Gamified Learning Stages: Present a series of interest-specific stages (like levels in a game), each consisting of a short video clip followed by a multiple-choice quiz.
- Interactive Video-Based Quizzes: Integrate short video clips relevant to the chosen interest, followed by multiple-choice questions based directly on the video content. Only one choice may be selected.
- Points Based Progression: Track user credits. Correct quiz answers awards points. hearts decrease by wrong answer, and are reset for new clips. Display points/credits, hearts (lives) at the top corner. Reset points upon new game. Overall interest-based point value counts to provide overall score.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Energetic blue (#29ABE2) to evoke curiosity and a sense of exploration in learning.
- Background color: Light blue (#E0F7FA) for a calm and engaging learning environment.
- Accent color: Vibrant yellow (#FFDA63) to highlight interactive elements and positive feedback.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for readability and a friendly feel.
- Use icons related to learning and gaming to illustrate interests and gamified progression.
- Employ a clean, intuitive layout with clear visual hierarchy, making it easy for users to navigate through quizzes and learning stages.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to provide visual feedback and enhance the gamified learning experience.
